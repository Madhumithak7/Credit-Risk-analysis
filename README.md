# Credit-Risk-analysis
# Credit Risk Analysis Portfolio — Standard Chartered Credit Analyst Simulation


## Overview

This repository documents a full credit analyst workflow completed as part of the **Standard Chartered Credit Analyst Job Simulation** (via Forage), assessing the creditworthiness of a fictional client, **Green Solutions Manufacturing Ltd** — a UK-based manufacturer specialising in eco-friendly, sustainable drinkware.

The project mirrors a real annual credit review process: analysing financial statements, computing and interpreting key accounting ratios, researching industry and macroeconomic trends, and synthesising the findings into a client-facing recommendation.

**Why this repo exists:** I'm building a portfolio around data analysis, financial reporting, and quantitative decision-making as I move into data analyst / BI / credit & risk analytics roles. This project demonstrates end-to-end financial statement analysis, ratio interpretation, industry research, and clear written/verbal communication of findings to a non-technical stakeholder.

## Client Snapshot: Green Solutions Manufacturing Ltd

- **Industry:** Sustainable / eco-friendly drinkware manufacturing (reusable water bottles, coffee cups)
- **Headquarters:** United Kingdom
- **Reporting currency:** GBP (£), in thousands
- **Financial years reviewed:** FY22 (ended 31-Dec-22) and FY23 (ended 31-Dec-23)
- **Context:** Client is seeking additional credit to fund an accelerating sustainability/capex program

## Key Findings at a Glance

| Metric | FY22 | FY23 | Trend |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue | £32m | £54m | ▲ +71% |
| EBITDA Margin | 4.7% | 10.3% | ▲ Strong improvement |
| EBITDA Net Interest Cover | 2.08x | 17.53x | ▲ Much stronger |
| Leverage Metric (Debt/EBITDA) | 7.82x | 1.24x | ▲ Sharply deleveraged |
| Current Ratio | 1.88 | 1.38 | ▼ Weakened liquidity |
| Free Cash Flow | £(3.2)m | £(8.0)m | ▼ More negative (capex-driven) |

**Overall assessment:** The client's credit profile has strengthened materially — driven by strong revenue growth, expanding margins, and a sharply reduced leverage position — supported by favourable long-term sustainability trends in its industry. The primary risk to monitor is short-term liquidity and negative free cash flow, driven by an aggressive capital expenditure program funding the client's sustainability initiatives.

**Recommendation:** Proceed favourably with the credit review, subject to a working capital breakdown and confirmation of the client's financing plan for ongoing capex.

## Tools & Methods Used

- **Excel** — financial statement modeling, ratio computation (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow Statement)
- **Financial ratio analysis** — liquidity (current ratio), profitability (EBITDA/operating margin), leverage (leverage metric, net leverage, interest cover), cash flow analysis (FCF, CF/CapEx)
- **Industry & macroeconomic research** — market sizing, growth drivers, competitive landscape, regulatory environment, GDP/inflation/interest rate analysis
- **Written & verbal communication** — structured preliminary report and stakeholder video presentation script
