# 01 — Financial Health Analysis

## Objective

Analyse Green Solutions Manufacturing Ltd's Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement for FY22 and FY23 to assess financial health and identify credit risks, using key accounting ratios across liquidity, profitability, and leverage.

## Process

1. **Reviewed the three core financial statements** (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow Statement) provided in the client's financial reporting workbook, covering the years ended 31-Dec-22 and 31-Dec-23.
2. **Computed key ratios** across four categories:
   - Profitability (EBITDA Margin, Operating Margin)
   - Liquidity (Current Ratio)
   - Leverage (Leverage Metric, Net Leverage, EBITDA Net Interest Cover)
   - Cash flow (Free Cash Flow, Cash Flow to CapEx)
3. **Interpreted each ratio's year-on-year movement** and what it signals about the client's credit risk.
4. **Compiled findings into a structured preliminary report** (see [`preliminary-report.md`](./preliminary-report.md)) addressed to the credit officer, including a risk assessment and actionable recommendations.

## Files in this folder

- [`preliminary-report.md`](./preliminary-report.md) — the full written preliminary report: executive summary, financial analysis, ratio breakdown, risk assessment, actionable insights, and conclusion.
- [`key-ratios-summary.md`](./key-ratios-summary.md) — a standalone reference table of every ratio computed, its formula, FY22/FY23 values, and a plain-English explanation of what it measures and why it matters for credit risk.

## Key Skills Demonstrated

- Financial statement literacy (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow Statement)
- Ratio-based financial analysis
- Translating quantitative output into credit risk narrative
- Structured business report writing
