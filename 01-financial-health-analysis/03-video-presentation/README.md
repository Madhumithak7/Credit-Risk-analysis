# 03 — Client Presentation (Stakeholder Video Script)

## Objective

Synthesise the financial health analysis (Stage 1) and industry/macroeconomic research (Stage 2) into a clear, concise presentation addressed to the credit officer, to support the annual credit review decision for Green Solutions Manufacturing Ltd.

## Process

1. Distilled the full preliminary report and industry research into a structured, spoken-word script — condensed for a ~60-90 second verbal presentation.
2. Structured the script around five sections: Introduction, Quantitative Analysis, Industry Research, Key Findings, and Conclusion (with a clear recommendation).
3. Prioritised clarity and decision-relevance over exhaustive detail — a real stakeholder presentation needs to lead with the "so what," not restate every number.

## Files in this folder

- [`presentation-script.md`](./presentation-script.md) — the final stakeholder-facing script.

## Key Skills Demonstrated

- Synthesising quantitative and qualitative analysis into a single coherent narrative
- Communicating financial findings to a non-technical/time-constrained audience
- Making a clear, defensible credit recommendation
