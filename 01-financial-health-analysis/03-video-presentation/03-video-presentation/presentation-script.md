# Video Presentation Script — Credit Review: Green Solutions Manufacturing Ltd

**Audience:** Credit Officer (Darren)
**Purpose:** Summarise financial health analysis and industry research to support an annual credit review decision.

---

**Good morning Darren,**

**Introduction & Purpose:**
This is my preliminary credit assessment of Green Solutions Manufacturing Ltd, covering the key financial ratios and industry trends behind my recommendation.

**Introduction to the Client:**
Green Solutions Manufacturing Ltd is a UK-based manufacturer specialising in eco-friendly, sustainable drinkware. FY23 revenue was £54m, up from £32m in FY22.

**Quantitative Analysis:**
Revenue rose 71% to £54m, and EBITDA margin expanded from 4.7% to 10.3%. Leverage fell from 7.82x to 1.24x, and interest cover rose to 17.53x — both strong signals. The one concern: the Current Ratio dropped from 1.88 to 1.38, and Free Cash Flow worsened to £(8.0)m, driven by an £8m capex spend.

**Industry Research:**
The drinkware market is growing 4-5% annually, with sustainability now table stakes — a good fit for this client. UK rates remain elevated at 3.75%, but the client's strong interest cover absorbs that.

**Key Findings:**
Credit profile has strengthened overall. Liquidity is the one area to monitor.

**Conclusion & Recommendation:**
Recommend proceeding with the credit review, with a follow-up on working capital. Thanks, Darren.

---
