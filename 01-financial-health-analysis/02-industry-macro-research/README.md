# 02 — Industry & Macroeconomic Research

## Objective

Broaden the financial statement analysis from Stage 1 with external context: research the sustainable drinkware industry the client operates in, and evaluate macroeconomic trends (GDP, interest rates, inflation, political/regulatory factors) that could affect the client's creditworthiness.

## Process

1. **Industry landscape research** — identified market size, growth rate, key competitors, and competitive dynamics in the global sustainable drinkware market (reusable water bottles and coffee cups).
2. **Industry trend analysis** — examined sustainability-driven demand shifts, regulatory tailwinds (single-use plastic bans), premiumization trends, and innovation trends (smart bottles, customisation, sustainable materials).
3. **Macroeconomic trend evaluation** — assessed UK GDP growth, Bank of England interest rate policy, and inflation trends, and connected each to the client's specific financial position (e.g., interest cover, cost pressures, demand sensitivity).
4. **Synthesis** — connected industry/macro findings back to the Stage 1 financial ratios to form a holistic view of credit risk (see [`industry-research-summary.md`](./industry-research-summary.md)).
